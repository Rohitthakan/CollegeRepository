Welcome To CollegeRepository

CollegeRepository, the ultimate destination for all your college exam needs. Our extensive database contains a wealth of old question papers for all branches of college, covering a variety of subjects. If you're a student looking for extra practice materials or last night study before exams, CollegeRepository has you covered.

What we are?

A bunch of innovative, enthusisatic and motivated college students, Who work together on CollegeRepository website. We will add new exciting features that will improve your user experience. Until then keep using and contributing.

What have we been occupied with?

CollegeSpace has been the one-stop solution for students the night before exam. It has a vast array of old question papers and We are working continously so that we can provide maximum material to the students.

Why should you visit CR?

Students who are preparing for exams may find it helpful to have access to past papers to practice with and get a sense of the types of questions that have been asked in the past and they can become familiar with the structure and format of the exam.
